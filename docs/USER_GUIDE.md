# KNX Club Jordan — User Guide

A support handbook for visitors and members of the KNX Club Jordan website.

---

## 1. About the Website

KNX Club Jordan is the online home of the professional KNX community in Jordan. It is a place for engineers, integrators, architects, students, and specialists working with smart-building and home-automation systems to meet, learn, share news, and join workshops and events.

The site is available in two languages:

- **Arabic** — the default language, displayed right-to-left.
- **English** — secondary language, displayed left-to-right.

You can switch languages at any time using the **EN / ع** button at the top-right corner of every page.

## 4. Browsing the Pages

### About
A short page describing the club's purpose and the three pillars: **Local expertise**, **Global standard**, and **Always current**.

### Services
A grid of six service cards explaining what members receive:

1. **ETS Training** — hands-on workshops using the official KNX engineering tool.
2. **Monthly Meetups** — recurring sessions for knowledge exchange.
3. **Joint Projects** — collaboration opportunities between members.
4. **Resource Library** — guides, templates, and reference material.
5. **KNX Secure** — guidance on secure KNX installations.
6. **Certification** — the path toward becoming a recognised KNX Partner or Tutor.

### Events
The **2026 Calendar** showing planned activities, for example:

- **ETS6 Fundamentals** — Spring 2026.
- **KNX Secure & IoT Workshop** — Summer 2026.
- **Automation Professional Day Conference** — Autumn 2026.

Events that have not yet opened for registration are marked **Coming soon**.

### Members
Two sections:

- **Founding board** — photos, names, roles, and companies of the founding members.
- **Partner companies** — partner organisations, each marked with a **KNX partner** badge.

### News
- **News list** — a grid of article cards showing the date, title, a short preview, and the cover image.
- Click any card to open the full article. Inside the article you will find the title, date, full text, the featured image, and a **Back to news** link to return to the list.

### Gallery
Two parts:

- **Pictures** — a grid of photo cards, each with a title and short description.
- **Videos** — a grid of videos (from YouTube, Vimeo, or hosted directly). Each video shows a title and description.

If the gallery is empty for any reason, you will see **"No items yet."**

### FAQ
Quick answers to the three most common questions:

1. **Is membership paid?** — Basic membership is free; paid tiers are planned.
2. **Do I need to be a certified KNX Partner?** — No. The club is open to all professionals and students interested in smart buildings.
3. **Which language are workshops delivered in?** — Mostly Arabic, with English technical terms.

## 5. Joining the Club

You can join from the home page (the **Join the club** section) or by clicking **Join** in the top menu.

**Fill the form:**

| Field          | Required? | Notes                                                          |
|----------------|-----------|----------------------------------------------------------------|
| Full name      | Yes       | Between 2 and 120 characters.                                  |
| Email          | Yes       | Must be a valid email address.                                 |
| Role           | Optional  | Example: integrator, engineer, architect, student.             |

**Submitting:**

1. Click **Sign me up**.
2. The button changes to **Submitting…** while the form is sent.
3. On success you will see a green confirmation: *"Thanks — you're on the list. We'll be in touch."*
4. You will also receive an auto-reply email from the club.
5. The club team is notified automatically and will follow up with you.

**Already signed up?** If you submit again with the same email, your name and role are simply updated — no duplicate record is created.

**Common messages you might see:**

| Message                                  | What it means                                  |
|------------------------------------------|------------------------------------------------|
| "Please enter your name."                | The name field is empty or too short.          |
| "Please enter a valid email."            | The email address is missing or malformed.     |
| "Role is too long."                      | The role text exceeds the allowed length.      |
| "Network error. Please try again."       | Your internet connection dropped — retry.      |
| "Server error. Please try again later."  | A temporary issue on the website — retry later.|

## 8. Tips & Troubleshooting

- **Page looks empty or images don't load** — refresh the page; if it persists, try a different browser or clear your browser cache.
- **The join form won't submit** — make sure your name and email are filled, and that the email looks correct (for example, contains "@").
- **You didn't receive the confirmation email** — check your spam/junk folder. If nothing arrives, email the club at the contact address above.
- **The site appears in the wrong language** — use the **EN / ع** toggle in the top-right corner.
- **A video does not play** — the video may be hosted on YouTube or Vimeo; check that those services are accessible from your network.
- **You want to update your details** — simply submit the join form again with the same email; the team will be notified.

## 9. Frequently Asked Support Questions

**Q: Do I need to create a password or an account?**
No. The club site is open to browse. To stay in touch you only fill the **Join the club** form once.

**Q: How will I be contacted after joining?**
By email, using the address you provided in the join form.

**Q: Can I attend events without being a member?**
Membership is recommended so you are notified of new workshops, but check the **Events** page for any open sessions.

**Q: Is there a mobile app?**
No mobile app is required. The website works on phones, tablets, and computers.

**Q: My information is wrong — can I have it removed?**
Yes. Email the club at the contact address and request removal or correction of your data.
